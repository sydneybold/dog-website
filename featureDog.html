Simcoe Samurai was born on Cinco de Mayo in 2020 to a rescued mix breed dog named Tokyo. Labs and More rescued Tokyo after she was abandoned and nurtured her back to heath. Simcoe has seven other siblings who all have Japanese names. As a puppy, Simcoe was adopted by Jimmy Gekas, a professional photographer and volleyball coach. He now lives in Carlsbad, CA. Simcoe spends most days hanging with his dad at Coast Volleyball Club chasing balls and entertaining the staff. On his days off, Simcoe enjoys hiking on the trails around Carlsbad and spending time at local craft breweries.
